Who is The Osea

The Osea is an aquatic museum where families and children experience and learn about marine life in a joyful, playful, and unforgettable way. Through interactive exhibits and immersive storytelling, The Osea guides visitors into the beautiful depths of the ocean making the marine life feel close, mysterious, and deeply important.

Design goals

The goal was to design a family-friendly website that mirrors the excitement of visiting an aquatic museum.

  1. Design interactive storytelling elements that mimic the experience of exploring exhibits.
  2. Use playful and expressive illustrations to connect with children.
  3. Include suprise animations for fun and playfulness.
  4. Optimized for all devices to ensure responsiveness.
