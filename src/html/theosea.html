The Osea

Year: 2025

Category: Musuem

Services: Brand identity / visual identity / web design / web development

Live site

Who is The Osea

The Osea is an aquatic museum where families and children experience and learn about marine life in a joyful, playful, and unforgettable way. Through interactive exhibits and immersive storytelling, The Osea guides visitors into the beautiful depths of the ocean making the marine life feel close, mysterious, and deeply important.

Design goals

The goal was to design a family-friendly website that mirrors the excitement of visiting an aquatic museum.

  1. Design interactive storytelling elements that mimic the experience of exploring exhibits.
  2. Use playful and expressive illustrations to connect with children.
  3. Include suprise animations for fun and playfulness.
  4. Optimized for all devices to ensure responsiveness.

Logo

The Osea logo was designed with a bubble background to reference the bubbles, subtly hinting the ocean's life. A soft pastel yellow is used for readability. The word “The” is styled with a subtle wave giving the logo movement related to an ocean's wave.

Colors

The colors for The Osea were selected to create an inviting and friendly tone that reflects their playful nature. The color palette combines soft accents with deep aqautic hues to bring the ocean to life.

  1. Primary Color (#074F6D) – Indigo dye: Used for backgrounds to simulate the ocean's feeling.
  2. Secondary Color #2 (#FAEDCD) – Papaya whip: A soft hue that balances with the darker tones. Used as a background color to contrast with the primary color.
  3. Accent Color (#A6E6F6) – Non photo blue: Used for subtitles to contrast against the primary color.
