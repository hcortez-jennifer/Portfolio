Who is Auclo?

Auclo is a luxury watch e-commerce brand that caters to watch enthusiasts, collectors, and those who appreciate timeless elegance and precision craftsmanship. The brand holds a selection of exquisite, high-end timepieces, providing customers with a seamless online shopping experience that reflects the sophistication and prestige of luxury watches.

Design goals

The goal was to create a premium online shopping experience that reflects Auclo's brand values.

  1. Elegant and clean design to showcase the sophistication of watches.
  2. Easy navigation and smooth browsing to enhance the shopping experience.
  3. Optimized for all devices to ensure responsiveness

Auclo

Logo

The serif typeface used in Auclo's logo was chosen to reflect the brand's essence of elegance, sophistication, and timeless style, aligning with their high-end watches. By implementing the serif typeface, it enhances Auclo's prestige image and it perceived a luxury brand.

Colors

The brand's color palette was chosen to reflect luxury, warmth, and sophistication.

  1. Primary dark (#B68D40) – Dark goldenrod: represents prestige and luxury, highlighting Auclo's high-end watches. Used for accents and call-to-actions.
  2. Primary Light (#E3D9D0) – Timberwolf: Ensures readability while maintaining a luxurious feel. Used for accents.
  3. Secondary Color (#F4EBD0) – Parchment Enhances the warmth and timeless feeling providing contrast against the dark primary colors. Used for backgrounds.
