My journey started with traditional art and progressed to different mediums, but I soon discovered a love for web design and development. When I'm not coding or designing, I enjoy drawing both digitally and traditionally, using pencil and charcoal to bring ideas to life. Whether I’m creating a website, sketching in charcoal, or exploring new design techniques, I’m always looking for ways to refine my skills and push my boundaries.
