The Osea

Year: 2025

Category: Musuem

Services: Brand identity / visual identity / web design / web development

Live site

Who is The Osea

The Osea is an aquatic museum where families and children experience and learn about marine life in a joyful, playful, and unforgettable way. Through interactive exhibits and immersive storytelling, The Osea guides visitors into the beautiful depths of the ocean making the marine life feel close, mysterious, and deeply important.

Design goals

The goal was to design a family-friendly website that mirrors the excitement of visiting an aquatic museum.

  1. Design interactive storytelling elements that mimic the experience of exploring exhibits.
  2. Use playful and expressive illustrations to connect with children.
  3. Include suprise animations for fun and playfulness.
  4. Optimized for all devices to ensure responsiveness.

Logo

The Osea logo captures the museum’s playful and fluid energy combining geometric shapes with a hint of motion. Letterforms were refined to include bubble-like negative spaces inside the “e” and “a” representing air bubbles. The centerpiece of the logo is the “O”, shaped like an orca, a subtle yet powerful nod to one of the ocean’s most intelligent and emotional creatures. The orca “O” symbolizes exploration, empathy, and the connection between humans and marine life, setting the tone for the brand’s storytelling approach.

Colors

The colors for The Osea were selected to create an inviting and friendly tone that reflects their playful nature. The color palette combines aqautic hues with bright colors bringing the ocean to life.

  1. Primary Color (#007897) – Cerulean: Used for backgrounds to simulate the ocean's feeling.
  2. Secondary Color #2 (#FFF9C4) – Lemon Chiffon: A soft hue that balances with the darker tones. Used as a background color to contrast with the primary color.
  3. Accent Color #1 (#F883C2) – Persian Pink: Used for accents, buttons, and call-to-actions.
  4. Accent Color #2 (#CE93D8) – Wistera: Compliments Perisan Pink as a secondary accent color such as hovering states.
